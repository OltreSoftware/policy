Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-01

This Cookie Policy explains how Virtual Break uses cookies and similar technologies on virtualbreak.it.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be retrieved by a web server in the domain that placed the cookie. They are widely used to make websites work, improve user experience, and provide analytics.

Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary cookies: used only to provide the website and its basic functions. Virtual Break aims to avoid setting non-essential cookies unless you consent.
  • Analytics cookies (subject to consent): used to measure visits and performance (e.g., which pages are most and least popular) and to improve our content.

Google Analytics 4 (if enabled)

Provider: Google (Google Ireland Limited / Google LLC). These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website in aggregate.

  • _ga – used to distinguish users; typical expiry: 2 years.
  • _ga_<container-id> – used to persist session state; typical expiry: 2 years.
  • _gid – used to distinguish users; typical expiry: 24 hours (may be present depending on configuration).

For details, see Google’s documentation and privacy policy: Privacy • Cookies.

Cookie consent

Analytics cookies are activated only after your explicit consent (when a consent banner is present). If you choose Decline, analytics cookies will not be set.

How to manage cookies

You can change your browser settings to block or delete cookies. The following links may help:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

If you previously consented to analytics cookies on our site and wish to withdraw your consent, you can clear cookies for this site in your browser. If a consent banner is available, you can also use it to revise your choice.
